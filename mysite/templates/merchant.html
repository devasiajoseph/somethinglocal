How Do I Sign Up?

Sign up through our online form. After a brief review process, you will
receive our Terms of Use. Once you've read it and electronically signed it, email it back to us. We will then guide you through the storefront set-up
and maintainence process.

You will receive weekly emails about the progress of our site,
which launches on October 5th, 2012.

To get a head start on your storefront, begin photographing your
products in a digital format, and write your required materials:
  • About your store
  • Product description and prices
  • Your Return Policy
Let shoppers know that they will soon be able to create
online registries featuring your products. After the launch, we will
provide marketing materials so you can promote your site in-store.
For our premiere vendors, there is no upfront cost. Something Local
makes income only when you sell a product. After the launch we will
continue to offer a no-charge membership to vendors who signed up
initally, so get started now!
